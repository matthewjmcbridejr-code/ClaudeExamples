# LifeOS Product Brief

## Working name

LifeOS

## Product idea

A family management app for organizing household tasks, shared calendar visibility, shared assets, bookings, permissions, and eventually a home e-paper display.

## Target users

- household owner/admin
- adult family members
- children/dependents
- guests/helpers such as nannies, drivers, assistants, or house staff

## Problem

Family operations are spread across chats, calendars, reminders, notes, and verbal instructions. It becomes hard to know who owns a task, what is happening today, and which shared resources are available.

## MVP goal

Create a simple LifeOS foundation where a household can:

- create household members
- assign tasks
- view today's tasks and calendar items
- manage basic shared assets
- prepare a display-ready summary for an e-paper screen

## Non-goals for the first version

- fully autonomous agents
- complex smart home automation
- advanced payments
- production-grade mobile app store launch
- deep integrations before the core workflow is proven

## Success criteria

The first version is successful if the household can clearly see:

- what needs to happen today
- who owns each task
- what shared assets are booked
- what appears on the home display
